About Me

Stars, Planets, and Nebulas; these objects have always fascinated me growing up as a kid. Whether it be watching astronomy videos on Youtube or gazing up at the moon's cycle throughout the month, my interest in astronomy is fervent and my desire to learn more continues to grow. I am passionate and excited to explore the field of astrophotography and image beautiful images of the cosmos. In this website, I hope to share exciting images and updates in the astronomy community that will not only incite curiousity and sublimity, but also instill an interest in learning more astronomy and enjoying the universe that surrounds us all.
